Mamma Mia meaning"MY MOTHER!" in italiano
and equivalent to "OH MY GOD!" who denoting various kind of emotions: surprise,joy and happiness.

'Mamma Mia' was founded in 1990 by the siblings 'Luca and Elena Romano'
who wanted to share their love for Italian cuisine.
It all started with one phrase they heard countless times growing up: 'Mamma Mia!'
